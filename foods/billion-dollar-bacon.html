Billion Dollar Bacon

Billion Dollar Bacon takes the classic "Million Dollar Bacon" to the next level by adding more flavor, more crunch, and more indulgence. This version features a perfect balance of sweet, spicy, and savory, transforming regular bacon into an irresistible treat that’ll have you craving it over and over again. It's bacon, but with a VIP upgrade.

Ingredients

Please gather the following:

  • 1 lb thick-cut bacon
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons maple syrup
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • Fresh thyme sprigs for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Arrange the bacon in a single layer on the baking sheet.
  3. In a bowl, mix brown sugar, maple syrup, Dijon mustard, cayenne pepper, paprika, apple cider vinegar, and black pepper.
  4. Brush the bacon with the sweet-spicy glaze, ensuring it’s fully coated.
  5. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until crispy and caramelized, flipping the bacon halfway through.
  6. Garnish with fresh thyme sprigs, and serve hot for a decadent treat!
