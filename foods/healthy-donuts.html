Very Healthy Deep Fried Donuts

These light and fluffy donuts are deep-fried for a crispy exterior, yet they're made with healthier ingredients to keep things guilt-free. With a touch of sweetness and a light, airy texture, theyâ€™re perfect for satisfying your donut cravings without all the heaviness. A quick recipe to make your own healthier version of this classic treat!

Ingredients

Please gather the following:

  • 1 cup of whole wheat flour
  • 1/2 cup of almond flour
  • 2 tablespoons of coconut sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup almond milk
  • 1 large egg
  • A small tiny pinch of salt
  • Peanut oil for frying
  • A deep fryer

Steps

  1. In a bowl, combine whole wheat flour, almond flour, coconut sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the egg, almond milk, and vanilla extract.
  3. Gradually add the wet ingredients to the dry, mixing until smooth.
  4. Heat peanut oil in a deep fryer until boiling.
  5. Drop spoonfuls of dough into the oil, frying for 2-3 minutes until golden brown.
  6. Remove and drain on paper towels, then serve warm with a dusting of cinnamon or a drizzle of honey.
